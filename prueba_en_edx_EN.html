Participant Support

If you did not find an answer to your questions in the General FAQ or Technical FAQ section, please fill out the form below so that we can help you as soon as possible.

When you click "Submit", a pop-up window will notify you that the form has been submitted and you will receive a notification in your e-mail box with the order number. Also, check your junk e-mail folder.

Certificates
The Inter-American Development Bank offers all its courses in verified certificate modality and in assistant modality. To receive a verified certificate it is necessary to make the established payment for each course and pass it with a minimum of 65% before the deadline established in the course.

In the assistant mode, the course can be taken completely free of charge. Although certificates are not awarded for those enrolled in this mode, you will be able to print your progress page to show that you have successfully completed the course.

In the past, edX has also offered "Honor Code" certificates. However, this option no longer exists. If you were part of a course that offered an "Honor Code" certificate, you will still have access to it in your edx.org dashboard.

To get more information about verified certificates, visitthe verified certificates page.

If you still have questions, clickcreate a Ticket.

Record of hours
The Inter-American Development Bank issues a certificate of hours that must be accompanied by the corresponding verified certificate. The certificate of hours is available in the "Course Material" section, in the right column of the course home page.

All courses of the IDBx Program of the Inter-American Development Bank can be taken free of charge. Although certificates are not awarded for those who are enrolled for free, you will be able to print your progress page to show that you have successfully completed the course.

If you still have questions, click on create a ticket.

Payments
The Inter-American Development Bank does not manage payments on the edX platform. To make a payment you must enter your “Dashboard” by clicking on your name in the upper right corner of the edX page and clicking the "Upgrade to Verified"in the corresponding course. Once this is done, you can make the payment with a credit card or PayPal. If you have a coupon code, remember to use it on this screen.

If you're having trouble making a payment, please contact info@edx.org.

To learn more about verified certificates, visit the verified certificates page.

If you still have questions, click Create a ticket.

WARNING: If you choose "Other issues", we will take longer to manage the request.
